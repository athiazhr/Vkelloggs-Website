VKellogg's Recipe - Crunchy Cereal Parfait

Enjoy Your Crunchy Cereal Parfait!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Kellogg's Corn Flakes
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/4 cup granola

Instructions:

  1. In a serving glass or bowl, layer Kellogg's Corn Flakes at the bottom.
  2. Add a layer of Greek yogurt on top of the Corn Flakes.
  3. Top the yogurt with a layer of mixed berries.
  4. Drizzle honey over the berries.
  5. Sprinkle granola on top for extra crunch.
  6. Repeat the layers if desired, ending with a final layer of granola.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
